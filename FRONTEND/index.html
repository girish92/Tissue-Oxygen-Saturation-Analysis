Mortality Prediction Model

The objective of the experiment is to determine associations between tissue oxygen extraction and mortality using time-series analysis and machine learning classification models. We also aim to determine how other confounders such as pH, hemoglobin, lactate and temperature impact the mortality and observe patterns in their trends for sepsis patients.
